Private Labeling

Brands throughout the world are using private label fragrances as a powerful way to reinforce their brand message. Fragrance has become one of the most attractive retail categories because it delivers a comprehensive brand experience.

Here are just a few of the key benefits private label fragrances provide

  • A powerful sensory connection back to your core brand. A daily touch point for your brand
  • High initial and maintained margins
  • High turns relative to other retail categories
  • A product that can be both a planned purchase or impulse buy
  • Long product life cycles
  • Extension opportunities into other beauty product categories
